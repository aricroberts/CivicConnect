Constituent advocacy tools & platforms

Found a bill you care about? These tools help you reach your representatives directly — with a script, a written message, or a text from your phone.

Contact your representatives
5Calls Calls

Scripted call guides and direct office numbers for active legislative issues. Enter your location — 5Calls finds your reps and walks you through the conversation.

5calls.org
Resistbot SMS / Chat

Text RESIST to 50409. Converts your message into a letter, fax, or call delivered to your federal and state representatives — in under two minutes. No script provided; you write your own.

resist.bot
Democracy.io Messaging

One form that emails all your federal legislators simultaneously. Backed by EFF developers. No account required — just your address and your message.

democracy.io
Track bills & legislation
Congress.gov Official

The Library of Congress's tracker for all federal legislation, floor schedules, committee activity, and voting records. Always current, always authoritative.

congress.gov
GovTrack Tracking

Independent federal bill tracking with voting records, passage probability scores, missed-vote rates, and email alerts for legislation you care about.

govtrack.us
OpenStates State Bills

Track bills and legislators across all 50 state legislatures — for when the issue is happening closer to home. Operated by Plural Policy; free bill tracking still available.

openstates.org
LegiScan State Bills

Impartial, nonpartisan bill tracking across all 50 state legislatures plus Congress. Custom tracking lists, email alerts, and session calendars for 2026.

legiscan.com
